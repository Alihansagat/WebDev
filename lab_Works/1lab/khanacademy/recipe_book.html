Recipe

  • Time: 20–25 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Rice 1 cup
Water 2 cups
Tablespoon vegetable oil 1 cup
Tablespoons ketchup 3 cups
Tablespoons sugar 2 cups
Tablespoons vinegar 2 cups
Tablespoon soy sauce 1 cup

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice and cook it in water until soft. Set aside.
  2. In a bowl, mix ketchup, sugar, vinegar, soy sauce, water, and cornstarch.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. (Optional) Add chopped vegetables and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Pour the sauce into the pan and stir until it thickens.
  6. Add the cooked rice and mix well.
  7. Cook for 1–2 more minutes, then remove from heat
